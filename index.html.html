About Me

I am a Georgia based designer pursuing a career in the field of UX/UI design. I currently provide graphic design services at Empire Arts Gallery in Decatur, GA. I help develop concepts and designs for clients including printed deliverables and consumer products. One of my favorite projects I had the pleasure to work on was creating and developing designs for the interior of Taco Cat, a 70's retro themed taco restaurant located in Augusta, GA. UX/UI has become an interest and passion of mine, seeing how design can be expanded into a wide variety of digital media
